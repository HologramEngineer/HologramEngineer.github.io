About me

I'm an professional Unity gameplay, UI and tools engineer open to contracting work based in Central Europe (Slovakia). I have masters degree from Technical University of Košice in Intelligent systems, focused on robotics and artificial intelligence, and have commercial programming experience working as game developer in Slovakia based mobile games studio Inlogic Software.

I work at the studio since 2016 as general developer, taking care of gameplay programming, UI, effects, game optimization, implementation of partner SDKs, ad services, in app purchases, and later online multiplayer for a live service game. I worked as solo engineer along with game and graphics designers on most of my projects. All games I worked on were at one time released commercially, either on Google Play / Apple App store, or sold to third parties.
